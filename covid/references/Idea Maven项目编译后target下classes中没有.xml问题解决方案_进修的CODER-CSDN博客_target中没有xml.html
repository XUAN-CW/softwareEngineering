解决方案：maven的pom.xml加上如下代码，将资源发布到编译类路径中.

  1. <build>
  2.         <resources>
  3.             <resource>
  4.                 <directory>src/main/java</directory>
  5.                 <includes>
  6.                     <include>**/*.xml</include>
  7.                 </includes>
  8.                 <filtering>true</filtering>
  9.             </resource>
  10.         </resources>
  11. </build>
